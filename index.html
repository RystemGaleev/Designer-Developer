Tips for developers & designers

Useful tips worth listening to

Ronald Richards
Developer
Leave an unhealthy workplace

If you constantly face negativity at work, if you can't find a common language with the management — look for a new job. If you expect that everything will settle down over time, do not forget that people usually do not change. Remember that you should not waste your talent. Find those who will appreciate you and your skills.
Eugene Spencer
Developer
Try to keep your knowledge and skills up to date.

This is easier said than done, but in order to keep your knowledge and skills up to date, you need to constantly learn and improve. Read articles and books, attend conferences, watch videos, subscribe to newsletters, attend training courses. There is a whole sea of opportunities in front of you to keep yourself in good professional shape.
Hugo Wilson
Designer
Know your worth

I always remind myself that I am worth the money I take from the client, or the money my employer pays me. All the knowledge and skills you have acquired, all the hours invested in programming, all your invaluable personal qualities should give you confidence in your abilities when negotiating financial issues.
